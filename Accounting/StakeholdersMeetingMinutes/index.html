Gratia Project Quarterly Meetings

The goal of Stakeholder/Providers/Contributors meetings is a meeting of representatives of the gratia users, contributors, core project, and providers to discuss and review the status of the project, the requests for enhancements, establish priorities in the ongoing activity and communicate results. This meetings will be held quarterly. The date/time TBD.
