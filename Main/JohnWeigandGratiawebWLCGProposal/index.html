Background

The current gratiaweb WLCG reporting pages display incorrect information and in several cases questionable information. These are the pages in question:
  • http://gratiaweb.grid.iu.edu/gratia/jot_reporting (note that this one brings up the previous month by default)
  • http://gratiaweb.grid.iu.edu/gratia/wlcg_reporting

The purpose of this document is to propose a change to the above pages in order to simplify and provide more relevant data than is currently being displayed.

  • WLCG Reporting (detail)
    This page will not display any pledge information.
    It will only display the data collected from OSG sources at the level collected (resource group, resource, service, etc) in order to facilitate troubleshooting.

  • WLCG Reporting (overview)
    This page will display the pledge information with the WLCG Reporting (detail) page data summarized to the pledge level.

IMPORTANT: The current pages (detailed at least) show pledge data for Installed Capacity and Availability/Reliability. I think it would nice to show all the metrics on these pages but there are other emails/reports generated showing these. DO WE WANT TO DUPLICATE THEM HERE OR KEEP IT SIMPLE FOR NOW AND ONLY SHOW THE ACCOUNTING DATA?

One thing we could do, for the other pledges, is just put links in to the WLCG pages for them ... or to the OSG url if that is more appropriate for now.
