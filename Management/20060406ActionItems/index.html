Both had to get up to speed on grids/VDT/accounting etc. Proposal for working deployment meeting at Wisconsin after Condor week. What is OSG policy on publishing OSG accounting information to EGEE and WLCG - different from US ATLAS/US CMS policies for this of course.
  5. WLCG and EGEE upgrade to current VDT version. Issues with GridFTP? and lcas/lcpmaps callouts compared to prima/gums needs to be clearly understood.
