3.1.1 Metrics Analysis

Summary:

All of the activities map onto the WBS areas:

  • 3.1.1.1 Metrics requirements, definition, and liaison with existing information sources
  • 3.1.1.2 Data mining and filtering and derived metrics data management
  • 3.1.1.3 Creation of OSG intelligence and key analytics
  • 3.1.1.4 Communicate interpretations in various forums (reports, blog, newsletter articles)

We have discussed metrics analysis and the more broadly scoped plans for "Assessment" in OSG at the March OSG Council meeting:

Other

We have been following and facilitating where possible issues relating to measured delivery of CPU resources. In particular the meaning and automated collection of normalized CPU in HEPSpec2006 units. This will require eventually cross-cutting work with Accounting, Interoperability and possible modifications to the Glue schema.
