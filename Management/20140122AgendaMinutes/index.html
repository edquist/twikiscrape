Top Concerns

  • The backlog of useful work is still significant and continues to grow. We will find a better way to track, understand, and manage this backlog.

  • We need to define and implement a basic disaster recovery plan. I keep saying this… so maybe after the Globus Toolkit updates are complete (which they nearly are), I will ask Mat to start on this task.

  • There has been little to no effort put into documentation lately. We are shifting the responsibility for this area back to Software and will draft a plan to address missing, incomplete, and out-of-date documents.

  • We seem to be tracking upstream software changes tolerably well, even without a systematic process. Some risk of missing an important update remains, but coverage is good enough that there may be little to gain by being more comprehensive.
