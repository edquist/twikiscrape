OSG Monthly Newsletters:

OSG distributes bimonthly newsletters in email to the mail list osgnews@opensciencegrid.org. If you are interested to be added to the distribution please email osg-webmaster@opensciencegrid.org

The aim of the newsletter is to provide members, staff, contributors, partners, and sponsors information about the work, accomplishments, events and people of the OSG.

More recent newsletter articles are available as part of the OSG News system
