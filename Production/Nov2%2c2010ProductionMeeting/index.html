Grid Operations Center (Rob Q.)

Operations Last Week

  • BDII instability event on 26/Nov. The problem was traced back to a IP renumbering project which had unforeseen effects on our internal DNS and LDAP. As soon as the issue was identified, the maintenance was stopped and service was rolled back to the pre-maintenance configuration. This was complicated by the need to roll back several RPM packages.
