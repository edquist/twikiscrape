Computing/Storage

  • Only EGI will be de-commisioning LCG-CE's
    • WLCG in EGI - (subject to Management Board decisions)

  • For WLCG sites that have misconfigured CREAM CE's open GGUS tickets
  • OSG is working to make CREAM CE work for OSG

  • CREAM Client - Maarten has emailed Anthony the location of a stand-alone CREAM client to test out. This will help glideinWMS <--> CREAM testing.
