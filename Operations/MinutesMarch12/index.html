Hot Topics for the week

1) OSG Site Characteristics and Capabilities Survey John Urish

An email was sent out this morning to all site administrators. Talked to Burt today and Eileen on Friday and they'll fill out surveys. They'll be calling administrators for the information. The goal is to obtain accurate information on what the sites report and an accurate view of what resources are available on the OSG. The survey should help us identify which sites are reporting information by matching up the responses with what is registered in the registration database. Ruth suggested that site administrators should be encouraged to come to the Operations Meeting with any discussions regarding the survey.
