6) Dealing with unresponsive sites.

  1. When does a site get removed from the OSG for lack of response?
  2. What steps should a support center take to contact a site, what steps should the GOC take?
  • An incident occurred recently where a USATLAS site become unresponsive to the Support Center's requests. So if a site becomes unresponsive, what policies do SC's have in place to act upon such cases? In the case of the KNU resource, the GOC removed the registration and all monitoring for the resource after several attempts were made to reach the site administrator. Doug suggested that the time period be three months, however, this seems long to some and Rob suggested six weeks. Murali suggested that after six weeks the monitoring be removed, and then in six more weeks, remove the registration, effectively wiping the site from the OSG. The final decision will be taken up with the Executive Team, but the GOC wants to receive feedback from SC's on how they deal with unresponsive sites. Three months would allow a very large window in the event of vacations or reorganizations, etc. The SC should make the determination to remove the unresponsive sites and contact the GOC so that further action can be taken.
