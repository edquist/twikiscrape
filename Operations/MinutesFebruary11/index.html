Hot Topics for the week

1. CEMon/BDII Aggregator Problems
Started sometime over the weekend, most of the data we have are about 66 hours old. GOC is actively working on it. Services were restarted in the AM, but still experiencing issues. Burt reports CMS has been unable to push data since friday around 5pm CST, problems with transfers, etc. isgrid on port 14001 is refusing connections, and there is stale data for a handful of resources. GOC is currently investigating and will send notifications

2. RSV collector crash Thursday, Feb. 7. Update
Brief outage to the RSV collector. The collector died due to a possible change at the SAM side. SAM folks confirm this and jobs backed up on the collector until things crashed. It is now back up and fixed, records are flowing. No data was actually lost, just delayed and queued locally. Email was sent to resources that were affected. Once the server came back up, we were not receiving data as the automatic restoration of record sending did not happen on certain resources. This is normally not the case as in the past the collector was brought down and things started again just fine.. Email was sent to admins requesting them to restart rsv
