Operations Service Level Agreement (SLAs) Catalog

About This Document

OSG Operations will define service level expectation through a series of SLAs.

All service level agreements will be between the OSG Operations and the OSG Stakeholders. Version control will be handled by the OSG TWiki and may differ from stakeholder to stakeholder.

Specific Service SLAs

Operational Response

  • GOC Service Desk

Critical Priority

  • BDII
  • MyOSG
  • OASIS, stratum 1 replica.

High Priority

  • CA Distribution includes OSG Software Cache
  • RPM Repository includes CA Certs and Middleware Cache
  • RSV including WLCG RSV to SAM Reporting
  • Resource Selection Service - Draft (4-28-10)

Normal Priority

Services Used by Multiple VOs

  • PerfSonar
  • OIM
  • OSG Display
  • Gratia - Draft
  • Ticketing Web Interface and Notification Services
  • WLCG Comparison Reports
  • Trash/Trash/Integration BDII
  • GOC FP Ticketing
  • Glide-In Factory
  • GOC Condor Collector
  • Retired Service - DOE Grid Certificate Services
  • OASIS, stratum 0 and login node.

Submission Services

  • XD-LOGIN
  • OSG Connect

Routine IT Services

  • TWiki
  • Document Repository
  • OSG Web Pages (Located at www.opensciencegrid.org)
  • JIRA
  • CILogon
