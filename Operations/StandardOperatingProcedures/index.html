Standard Operating Procedures for OSG Operations.

About this Document

Operating procedures are initiated by some type of event that causes or motivates an action to occur. The main purpose of the documents are to guide the human actions an illustrate the workflow. The procedures below have been approved by the Support Centers Technical Group. Draft procedures under development are at DevelopingStandardOperatingProcedures.

Developing Standard Operating Procedures

Procedures for GOC and Support Center staff contributing to OSG Operations need to be documented, aproved, published, and followed, to make a smooth and efficient operation for OSG, and to enable a process for improving operations.
