Hot Topics for the week

1) OSG usage spotlight on USCMS

  • Big physics experiments starting at the LHC at CERN. International resources: 7 tier 2s, 2 tier 1 at Fermilab. Rio and Brazil tier 2s in S. America. Simulation effort has only been running at CMS-branded sites. Simulated and real data coming next year.

2) Discuss the technical aspects relating to the RSS Feeds.

  • Some readers don't like the feed link. Various RSS readers handle the feeds differently. Safari, Firefox 2.0, Thunderbird and other software the GOC has tested read the link, but some readers may not work.

3) PBS security concern and available updates.

  • PBS Pro and Open PBS had the same problem. A fix has been released in the newer versions/releases. If sites are running PBS, they should patch these installations by getting a newer, patched version. Support Centers should be contacting sites and asking them to patch their systems.
