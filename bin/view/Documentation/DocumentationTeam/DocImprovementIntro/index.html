Slide 5: Workshop Goals

  1. Review processes, tools, and plans
  2. Train this group in use of the above
  3. Get high level understanding of quality of existing documents
  4. Set up tracking table for Area Owners and Document Owners
  5. Get experience with tools by improving a document

Slide 6: Set Direction for Next Steps

  1. Cleanup of orphan documents
  2. Moving all official documents into ReleaseDocumentation
  3. Organizing documents
  4. Identify Owners for all official documents, Reviewers, Testers as appropriate
  5. Improving documents
