The Plan

  1. Jim will move the documents indexed by RPMTempDocuments to Release3 over the next week (by Fri, Sept. 30). Some will be renamed as appropriate but the index will remain valid so anyone on the software team will be able to find them and evolve in the meantime or after the move. The owners of those documents will be responsible for integrating additional information copied from the ReleaseDocumentation web install documents which will not be moved to Release3. (Or if the owner wants to copy the current document and update it with the RPM changes, he/she can do that.)
  2. Jim and Robert will tag documents in ReleaseDocumentation that can be moved with no changes. After that list is reviewed by the software team, GOC will mass move those to Release3.
  3. Documents in ReleaseDocumentation requiring minor change (e.g. VDT_LOCATION) will be identified and the owners of those documents will have to copy them to Release3.
  4. The list of all owners will be created by Robert and Jim. They will go through the list of owners and verify/change to correct owners where known. The remaining documents/owners will be distributed to Alain (or the software team) for update.
