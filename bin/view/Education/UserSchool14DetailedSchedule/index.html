The schedule is mostly complete at this time, but there may be small changes as we finish all of the details.

All events happen in the Computer Sciences building, Room 1240, except when shown in orange below.

Sunday, 6 July 2014

Welcome Dinner for Students and Staff

Location Memorial Union (800 Langdon Street), room TBD
Time 6:30 p.m.

Tim and Lauren will meet students in the lobby of the DoubleTree, starting around 5:50 p.m. At about 6:10 p.m., we will take all of the students who meet there over to the Memorial Union. It is about a 10-minute walk. Join us if you want, or otherwise meet us in the dinner room by 6:30 p.m.
