Lecture and Lab Schedule

TO REVISE: The workshop will run from 9:00 am to 6:00pm (8:00 AM Registration on Saturday), with a one hour lunch break. Each day will consist of four lectures (of approximately 30-45 minutes), each followed by longer hands-on laboratory time (60-90 minutes). Lecturers and assistants will provide assistance during lab sessions and be available for discussion and follow-up.
