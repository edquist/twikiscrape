2006

Summer Grid Workshop 2006 The Summer Grid Workshop 2006 was sponsored by the Center for Gravitational Wave Astronomy (CGWA), the Grid Physics Network (GriPhyN) and the International Virtual Data Grid Laboratory (iVDGL) and presented a one-week intensive Summer Workshop program in grid computing and its application in scientific data analysis.

2005

Summer Grid Workshop 2005

The University of Texas at Brownsville and Texas Southmost College (UTB/TSC), the Grid Physics Network (GriPhyN), the International Virtual Data Grid Laboratory (iVDGL), and the Grid Research Trash/Trash/Integration Trash/Deployment and Support Center (GRIDS) sponsored a one-week intensive summer workshop program in grid computing and its application in scientific data analysis. This school was held on South Padre Island, a resort located on the Gulf of Mexico, twenty miles from downtown Brownsville, Texas during the week of July 11, 2005 - July 15, 2005
