Becoming a Reviewer

  1. Register for a userID on this wiki. If you have an ID but have forgotten your password, [[TWiki.ResetPassword][reset your password].
  2. If you are not already listed, click Edit Table below the appropriate table and add your name to the reviewer list for that lab tutorial.
  3. Join the education-content listserv.
  4. Announce that you are reviewing this particular lab tutorial.
  5. Receive the current password for the gridlab servers.
  6. Review your lab tutorial.
  7. Announce to the list again when you have completed your review.

Review the Lab Tutorial

Go through the tutorial, working it just as if you were in the class. If you find an error, you can correct it yourself by editing the wiki topic. Just click Edit at the top of the page. If you are not comfortable editing TWiki pages, just use one of the Comment boxes to leave a comment, explaining what you found. The comment box will insert your userID and the date automatically.

What to review for:

  • Simple errors
  • Did this make sense to you?
  • Were you able to move on to the next lab with what you learned?

Update the Review Table

Click the Edit Table button at the bottom of the appropriate review table. Edit the table contents to reflect that you have reviewed the lab or presentation.
