South Africa

We are working with a group of faculty and graduate students from the Witwatersrand (Wits) University and University of Johannesburg, South Africa and Columbia University to establish an OSG cluster at Wits/UJ (University of Johannesburg) and to involve scientists in using it for their research.
An update (as of Feb 2009) on the SA activity can be found here.

Training and education events organized by OSG in partnership with South African team:

  • Easter Research Cluster Training Workshop, April 9-14, 2009 in Johannesburg, followed by post-workshop assistance on porting application to the the newly established UJ cluster.
  • South African Grid School July 23-25, 2008 in Johannesburg

South America

Scandinavia
OSG's international outreach and education activities with Scandinavia include three education activities addressed to different groups of young researchers and groups that collaborate with OSG:
  • Interoperation of the NorduGrid Distributed Facility and Open Science Grid
  • Application support for the IceCube collaboration
