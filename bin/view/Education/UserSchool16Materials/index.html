Monday Morning: Introduction to HTC

  • Lecture: Introduction to HTC (PowerPoint) (PDF)

  • Exercise 3.1: A job that needs retries
  • Exercise 3.2: A brief detour through the Mandelbrot set

  • Exercise 3.4: A more complex DAG
