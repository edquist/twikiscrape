Minutes

Attendees: Brian L., Ken, Kyle, Mat, Rob Q., Scott, Shawn, Susan, Tim C., Tim T.

Security

  • Of the three recent vulnerabilities that affected OSG, the Singularity update was notable for how well the coordination between the developer and OSG worked. In contrast, the Slurm release was not as well coordinated, and so Susan plans to get to know the Slurm development team better to improve future security-related releases there.
  • Current projects are going well
  • Main concerns have to do with personnel and effort changes from Year 5 to Year 6. The Security Area is working hard to make sure that institutional knowledge is preserved through the transition. One bonus is that there may be some contributed effort of past Security Team members for a bit longer to ease the transition. Also, there is recognition that fewer total FTEs in the Security Area means that there will be less effort available to large projects. On the other hand, they hope to realize some efficiencies by automating routine tasks.

Action items
