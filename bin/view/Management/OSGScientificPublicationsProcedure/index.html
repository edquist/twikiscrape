Updating and keeping track of times that OSG is referenced in writings and posting them to the OSG Scientific Publication Page.

Scope

The OSG administrator staff will work with Chander Sehgal and Ruth Pordes to keep the scientific publication page current and up to date.

Publication Notification

The administrative staff will work with Dave, Kent and Ruth to keep track of any publications that reference OSG. The administration staff will send email to the above parties every 3 months to check the status of any new publications. We will also be notified via email if a publication is mentioned. For further confirmation of the definition of the different types of publications please view the: Scientific Publication Page

Prior to Posting a Publication

Once you are notified of a publication, if you are unsure if it is accurate please send email to marcia@fnal.gov for confirmation. Once this is confirmed you will post the new publication at Scientific Publication Page under one of the three following categories :
  • Scientific Publication
  • Publication of the OSG and related projects
  • OSG papers for General Reference

Posting a Publication

Before editing please make sure that the referenced publications are listed by Name & Year, located under the correct category, does not currently exist and saved when completed.
