OSG RA Items

1) Personal certificate renewals. Should the renewal notifications cease once the certificate is renewed?

  • No, they will continue for 30 days, as the mail states. If you revoke the old one or write to osg-ra they can revoke it for you. It was suggested that the mail include instructions on revoking the old one, but that shouldn't been done before 24 hrs has expired after the renewal or the user's jobs could fail due to outdated grid-mapfiles.

2) There is an update coming to the DOEGrids CA that will require a change of some of the scripts currently in use. There is no date set for this change (perhaps a couple of weeks before the transition begins) but the GOC would like to send out some reminders about the change so that everyone has ample notification about the event.
