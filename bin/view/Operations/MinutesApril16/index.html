3) Streamlining maintenance information

Burt brought up that administrators have a lot of places to announce site maintenance. An enhancement has been requested to limit this number by registering email addresses in the OSG maintenance tool so that it sends email to those addresses. Ongoing development of the tool is part of a larger project that ties into updates to the registration database and the next release is estimated to be the end of July.

4) Trash/Engagement is looking for 1000-1500 job slots for running the current application.

  • Review the VORS information
  • Review information from Chris Greens VO testing http://home.fnal.gov/%7Egreenc/site_tests/Engage_latest/
  • Problems Mats is encountering http://www.renci.org/~rynge/osg/
  • List of certified sites http://haw.renci.org/~rynge/condor_grid_overview.txt

A limited number of sites are supporting the Engage VO. USCMS has suggested that it will host the Engage VO jobs, so they can try to gain job slots at USCMS. The GOC will send out another email to the Support Centers requesting support of the VO. Issues were discussed regarding the use of home directories for storing large files when $APP and $DATA are preferred for storing these large files.

5) OSG VO migration from indiana.edu to voms.opensciencegrid.org located at Fermilab

Chander is the new OSG VO administrator. Commercial entities that are interested in being involved in the OSG are going to be managed through the OSG VO now hosted at Fermilab. The OSG VO will continue to also be hosted at IU for now.
