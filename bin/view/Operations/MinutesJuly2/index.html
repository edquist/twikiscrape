Hot Topics for the week

1) Highlights from the ATLAS Tier2 Conference two weeks ago

No one was available to go through the highlights this week. However, it was revealed that Tier 2 sites were required to upgrade by the end of June.

2) VO spotlight on STAR

The bundles they're running generate 200MB files which are sent back to the site via globus-rurl-copy....200-300 jobs per bundle. Not currently running. Raw data comes back and they submit it to the PDSF-NERSC site for production. They're working on a framework to monitor their efficiency, which they have determined so far is at 96%.

3) Upgraded to 0.6.0 status

Roughly 50 sites are now using 0.6.0. Are there any sites making plans to upgrade to 0.6.0?
