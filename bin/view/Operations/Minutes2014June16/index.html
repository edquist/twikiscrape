WLCG & EGI Operations

Security

  • No new major incidents or vulnerabilities. Security surveys should be getting created as twiki pages for individual area/system owners in the next day or two. Keep an eye out for email.
  • There have been an increasing number of bitcoin mining incidents detected in the research community. We have not experienced any such incident in OSG yet, but we are cautiously watching the incidents in other research infrastructures. The OSG security team would like to take the opportunity to remind you that OSG resources are only for scientific use and bitcoin mining activities are not allowed. We kindly ask you to emphasize this point to your community and ensure that your colleagues are clear on the OSG Accepted Use Policy. We expect VO managers to explain to their members the OSG AUP when a new user joins the collaboration. We would appreciate if you also send out a note to your community to remind them that such behavior will have consequences for the user, VO, and the resources.
