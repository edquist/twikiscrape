Hot Topics for the week

1) VO Spotlight on NYSGRID

A collaboration of institutes across the state, such as, Buffalo, Cornell, ARPI and Stoneybrook. One of the purposes is to make it easy for smaller institutions to use the OSG resources and allow them to participate even if they aren't providing resources. So there is a lot of collaboration with the smaller institutions. Enagement VO utilization is being used to get some training materials up. They have twelve contributors so far. There is an upcoming workshop mostly for technical users in NYSGRID. A goal is get everyone on 0.6.0 as well as cover the topic of how to port over apps to run on the grid and support those apps and users.

2) DZero Production Run Update

From Brad Abbott's email, D0 is essentially finished with its reprocessing of data. We have a handful of merging to do but this is small. Due to issues with making sure we do not duplicate jobs it may be a week or two before we are completely done, but we have less than 1% of the data to merge. See attachment at the end of the agenda for more information.
