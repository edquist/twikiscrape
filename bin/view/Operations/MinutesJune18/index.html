Hot Topics for the week

1) VO Spotlight on SBGrid

Eighty labs across country participating. Harvard Medical School in Boston is where the activity is centered. Currently there are two people working on the high performance computing side. They've been working with Condor for a couple of weeks and the installation is not yet complete. One of the questions they have is who is their support group? Rob explained their options: to use community support, form their own Support Center and support their users directly, or utilize support resources already being provided by another group. A CE is being brought up soon and they have been encouraged by the GOC to support the MIS VO and the GOC will run a series of site_verify tests on the site. SBGrid is not in the current VO package because they're still getting things up and running (VOMS server, for example). A lot of activity is anticipated in the next couple of weeks as they get their CE up and become more active in OSG.
