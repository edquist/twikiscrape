First agenda item

LIGO Binary Inspiral issues

Discussion

During the LIGO task force meeting it was reported that the submission node has experienced heavy swapping during file transfers and even though the test dataset was successfully transferred to several OSG sites the process doesn't seem reliable. Tanya has started to do some testing to understand memory issues. During the testing it was discovered that srm-client exits on its own if the duration of the transfer exceed 30 minutes (--connectiontimeout default). The developers are looking into the problems. We will try to have daily meeting with Britta until we were able to understand the issues and make the transfer workflow more reliable. Tanya and Doug would ask for login on the LIGO submission node.

Second agenda Item

Hadoop rpms

Discussion

Jeff have discussed hadoop rpms release process with Michael Thomas and Alain Roy. He has also contacted Abhishek to get access to rsync script that was used to synchronized vdt rpm repository with Caltech rpms. The small test stand is now functional at UCSD (hdfs (two namenodes) and two data servers). Jeff will work on bestman, gridftp rpms installation as soon as he gets more nodes. Tanya has asked about the process of bestman rpms release (itb version bestman 2.2.1.3.16, bestman2 2.0.3, 2.2.1.3.17, 2.0.4 will be released to itb soon ). Jeff will figure it out and let us know.
