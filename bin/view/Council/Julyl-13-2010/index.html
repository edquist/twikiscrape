AOB

  • Discussion and Request for input from Consortium: Community “X” is considered an OSG Major Stakeholder if the following apply:
    1. Has a major reliance on OSG assets for its science analysis
    2. OSG dedicates effort/reasources to helping Project “X” adapt its computing infrastructure to leverage OSG assets
    3. Is (or there are plans to become) a member of the OSG Council and invests in setting direction for OSG
    4. Serves as a proponent for OSG with agencies; conducts outreach to other science communities and academic institutions
    5. Via its own resources and work, creates assets that benefit other OSG stakeholders
    6. Commits to use and evolve the OSG principles and technical blueprint
