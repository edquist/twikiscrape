First agenda item

Gratia data quality.

Discussion

The following are potential gratia probes misconfigurations that should be looked at:
  • VO "unknown" - catch all for misconfigured sites ~ 308,960 wallclock hours for November 21st (compare with usatlas 408,800 and cms 410,993 hours on the same day). Include rsv probes and various people
  • There are also VOs like webportal, external , pilatlas , renci, glideinwms - looks like misconfigured pilot probes (50896 jobs for November so far, 28817 hours)
  • RSV reports are mapped various VOs (site owner VO, unknown VO, other VO, rsvuser VO etc)
  • VO "other" - discarded data , do we have a clear definition who should be mapped to this VO?
  • There are many individual users mapped to a VO== username (more then 200 users that are mapped to VOs that not registered in OIM since 2011, more then 10 in November )

Marco has provided guidance for RSV probes in the new installation guide - can somebody who understands the issue review it?

Rob Quick will discuss how to proceed with Keith and Steve. This is an operational issue.
