v0.34 Release Status.

After several false starts, the gratia backup was restored to gratia07. Problems (similar to those experienced with PostgresQL) with the SQL backup in that restoration is not done in dependency order. Fixed in 5.1 series but 5.1 is not in production yet.

During replication from gratia06 to gratia07 to catch up, missing records and inconsistent md5 checksums were were noticed. Initial indications are that the missing records are due to an inability of the 0.32 replicating system to deal with bad response from the replicatee which is already rectified in 0.34. Inconsistent checksums are urrently being investigated.
