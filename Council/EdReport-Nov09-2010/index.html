Executive Director Report for November.

OSG sites held our 2nd annual Campus Grid workshop http://indico.fnal.gov/conferenceDisplay.py?confId=3674 and made plans to extend the use of the glidein mechansism to extend campus resource sharing using flocking to non-condor batch systems.

Components of the GT5 toolkit including GRAM5 deployed from the OSG VDT software stack via native packaging are in initial test by the LIGO Scientific Collaboration (LSC).

The OSG Security Officer has been communicating with Galois, a small business, to connect them with representatives of the OSG VOs for a problem definition and requirements document.

Trash/Trash/Blueprint meeting on a variety of topics today and tomorrow https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Trash/Trash/Trash/Trash/Blueprint/BlueprintNovember

Report from the SI2 workshop is available https://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org:440/cgi-bin/RetrieveFile?docid=1002&version=1&filename=WorkshopReportSubmittedtoNSF.pdf

Discussions with CDIGS-2 leadership and NSF program managers on an OSG supplement for sustaining and support for 1 year.

Council chairs, Executive Team meeting this Thursday/Friday: EDs goal for the meeting is to come out of the meeting with a plan, framework and outline for writing and reviewing the OSG’ proposal to DOE ASCR/OHEP/NP and NSF MPS/OCI for the end of February. Executive Director is requesting a review of the proposal with external reviewers. LIkely to be the week of Jan 24th. Reviewers to include Science Advisory Group members, Ian Bird, both XD PIs, Steve Cotter, Kathy Yellick.

-- RuthPordes - 08 Nov 2010
