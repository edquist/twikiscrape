OSG Software Team

Discussed

  • HTCondor 8.3 still breaks HTCondor CE on EL 5 (SW-1921). BrianL has gathered and submitted a great deal of debugging information for this problem, but there has been no help from the Flightworthy team lately. TimC asked whether this problem will be addressed in the HTCondor 8.3 series. TimT said that he hopes so. Is there a Flightworthy ticket for this issue even? Is anyone on that team assigned to deal with it?
