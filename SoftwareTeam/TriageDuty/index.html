Software Team Triage Duty

Triage Rotation

We rotate triage duty for everyone whose total allocation to the Technology Area is greater than or equal to 50%, except Brian Bockelman.

Triage Script

  1. Update checkout (Documents/OSG/Software/Calendar/osg-triage-assignments)
  2. Generate next rotation 
    ./triage.py --generateNextRotation > rotation.txt
  3. Check and update assignments against calendar
  4. Load triage assignments into Google Calendar 
    ./triage.py --load rotation.txt

Triage Duty Calendar

The calendar is hosted on Tim Cartwright’s Google Calendar account. If you need privileges to edit, just ask Tim.

To subscribe to this calendar in your calendar program, use the iCal URL: https://www.google.com/calendar/ical/h5t4mns6omp49db1e4qtqrrf4g%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics

Weekly email

An email goes out every Monday at 6 a.m. (Central Time) with the names of the staff on triage duty that week and on the following week. The script that sends out the email lives in SVN as software/tools/email-triage-duty and there's a checkout in /p/condor/workspaces/vdt/tools from where a cronjob runs it. The cronjob runs as the cndrutil user on tonic.cs.wisc.edu which is a RHEL 5 machine at UW-Madison. It looks like this:

0 6 * * 1 /s/std/bin/runauth /usr/bin/python /p/condor/workspaces/vdt/tools/email-triage-duty -u 'https://www.google.com/calendar/ical/h5t4mns6omp49db1e4qtqrrf4g%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics' -e osg-software@opensciencegrid.org
