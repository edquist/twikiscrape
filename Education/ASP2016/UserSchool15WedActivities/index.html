Wednesday Afternoon Options

After Wednesday morning instruction and lunch, you are welcome to select any of the below activities, including striking out on your own. School instructors have organized special discussion sections and activities, but we've also listed some activities to pursue on your own.

Transportation and Costs

Please note that School-Organized Activities are all free and walkable. If you are interested in the On Your Own activities also listed, or prefer not to walk, you will be responsible for you own transportation costs and activity costs. We cannot reimburse for transportation costs outside of travel to and from the OSG User School.
