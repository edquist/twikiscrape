2015 OSG User School Schedule

The schedule is mostly complete at this time, but there may be small changes as we finish all of the details.

All events happen in the Computer Sciences building, Room 1240, except when shown in orange below.

Sunday, 26 July 2015

Welcome Dinner for Students and Staff

Location Memorial Union (800 Langdon Street), Tripp Commons
Time 6:30 p.m.

We will have instructors at hotel lobbies by ~5:55pm. At about 6:10 p.m., we will walk over to the Memorial Union, which will take 10-15 minutes, depending on the hotel you're in. Join us for the walk if you want, or otherwise meet us in the dinner room by 6:30 p.m.
